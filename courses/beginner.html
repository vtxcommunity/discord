Beginner

Every amazing community starts somewhere—and that somewhere is in a smart server structure, engaging your members, and understanding the basics of safety and moderation.

What you’ll know by the end of the course

  • Server foundations that help your members settle in and participate with ease
  • Crucial best practices to keep you and your community safe
  • Tricks to welcome and engage new members—one of the most important pieces of community success
  • How to start building your moderator team

Get to know the basics that will set you up for long-term success, especially how to set up your server's settings, channels, and permissions.
